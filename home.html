I’m Tamilselvi, Managing Partner at TSY TRIANGLE, when I was wrapping covers to school Text Books to my Kid, it took almost more than a couple of days, being a working mother, found very difficult to spend time to wrap books at the last minute, and also can’t able to balance work life and kid’s time.

I quickly discovered an idea of Wrapping Text books to the school students as a Business, with this idea, it helps the parents to save Time and also, we thought of helping needy college students financial support by involving them in to the project.

Academic year 2023-24, we implemented this project in few schools in and around annanagar, which was achieved great success between parents. As a result, we found many are in need of this service. So thought to bring up this as a project in the schools, it will be helpful to the parents when they receive Wrapped Text Books from the school.
